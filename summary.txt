Summary

ALUPA FOODS PRIVATE LIMITED has appointed MALLYA & MALLYA as their statutory auditor for a period of five years, commencing on April 1, 2022, and concluding on March 31, 2027. The appointment is effective from September 26, 2022.


Summary of the attachments

Here are the summaries of each document:

*   **Intimation Letter Signed:** Alupa Foods Private Limited informs Mallya & Mallya Chartered Accountants of their appointment as statutory auditors, as resolved at the Sixth Annual General Meeting, and requests confirmation and an engagement letter.

*   **Consent signed:** Mallya & Mallya Chartered Accountants provides their consent to act as statutory auditors for Alupa Foods Private Limited, confirming their eligibility and compliance with Section 139 and 141 of the Companies Act, 2013.

*   **Resolution for appointment of Auditor Signed:** This document certifies the resolution passed at Alupa Foods Private Limited's Sixth Annual General Meeting, appointing Mallya & Mallya Chartered Accountants as statutory auditors for a 5-year term, with remuneration to be agreed upon by the Board of Directors and Auditors.
